STM32 FLASHv1 driver.

Driver capability:

- Supports internal flash write as explained in ST's PM0075 (CD00283419).
- Currently does not support bank2 of STM32F1xx_XL devices.

Microcontroller support:

- STM32F101xx;
- STM32F102xx;
- STM32F103xx;
- STM32F105xx;
- STM32F107xx.

Tested only on a STM32F103_MD
